# Privacy Policy for LinuxTV Remote

**Effective Date:** April 5, 2026

## Introduction

LinuxTV Remote ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- None. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the App.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information:** We may collect basic device information such as device type, operating system version, and app version to ensure compatibility and improve the app's performance.
- **Network Information:** The App communicates with your local LinuxTV Desktop service over your local network. We do not collect or store any network data, IP addresses, or connection logs.

### Third-Party Services
The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

## How We Use Information

We use the limited information collected solely to:

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect any information collected against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
