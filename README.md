# LinuxTV

LinuxTV is a TV-first app launcher for Linux systems.

It turns a normal PC connected to a TV into a simpler, more living-room-friendly experience. Instead of dealing with a traditional desktop, mouse, tiny windows, and scattered apps, LinuxTV gives you a fullscreen home screen that is easier to navigate from the couch.

## What It Is

LinuxTV is meant for people who use a Linux machine as a media box, TV computer, kiosk-style entertainment system, or family-friendly big-screen setup.

It gives you:

- a clean fullscreen launcher for your apps
- fast access to streaming sites and media apps
- phone-based remote control
- a more appliance-like experience instead of a normal computer desktop

## Why People Use It

A normal desktop operating system is not comfortable to use on a TV.

Typical problems:

- icons and windows feel too small from a distance
- launching apps takes too many steps
- a keyboard and mouse are awkward in a living room
- browsers and media apps do not always behave well on a TV screen
- family members or guests may find a full desktop confusing

LinuxTV helps by simplifying the whole flow. You power on the machine, open what you want quickly, and control it in a way that feels closer to a TV interface than a workstation.

## How It Helps

LinuxTV is useful when you want your setup to feel:

- easier to navigate from across the room
- cleaner and less distracting
- better suited for media and streaming
- friendlier for non-technical users
- more consistent when opening apps in fullscreen

It is especially helpful for:

- home theater PCs
- family TV computers
- streaming setups
- media kiosks
- bedroom or lounge-room Linux boxes

## Main Features

- Fullscreen app launcher designed for TV use
- Support for both native apps and web apps
- Phone remote control for navigation and input
- Touchpad mode for cursor movement
- Keyboard mode for typing from your phone
- Faster directional navigation with long-press support
- Quick actions like Back, Home, Menu, Play/Pause, and Close App
- Power actions like Shutdown and Reboot from the remote menu
- A simpler "pick an app and go" experience

## The Experience

The goal of LinuxTV is not to feel like a desktop computer.

The goal is to make a Linux-powered TV setup feel more relaxed, more direct, and easier to control. It reduces friction between turning on the screen and getting into the app or service you actually want to use.

## In Short

LinuxTV helps turn a Linux PC connected to a TV into something that feels closer to a smart TV or media hub, while still keeping the flexibility of running your own apps and services.
