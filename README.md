# LinuxTV

**A powerful, freedom-first TV operating system that breathes new life into old hardware.**

LinuxTV is a custom operating system designed to transform any computer into a powerful TV entertainment hub. Similar to Android TV OS but with significantly more power, complete user freedom, and total control over your applications. Plug it in via USB, boot from any machine, and you are ready to go.

## The Vision

Every year, millions of perfectly functional computers are thrown away because they are too old for modern operating systems. LinuxTV fights this e-waste crisis by giving old hardware a second life as a fully-featured TV entertainment system. At the same time, it works beautifully on any machine, old or new, through a simple USB flash drive.

**You own your device. You control your apps. No restrictions, no bloat, no corporate gatekeeping.**

## Key Features

### 🔥 Complete User Freedom
* **Full control** over which apps you install and use
* **No forced applications** or pre-installed bloatware
* **Choose your own ecosystem** and run any Linux-compatible software
* **Privacy-first** design with no telemetry or tracking
* **Ad-free browsing** powered by Brave browser for a clean, uninterrupted experience

### 🌍 Sustainability & E-Waste Reduction
* **Revive old computers** that cannot run modern OS requirements
* **Minimal hardware requirements** and works on machines from 10+ years ago
* **Reduce electronic waste** by repurposing instead of replacing
* **Lightweight and optimized** for older hardware

### 💾 Universal USB Portability
* **Boot from any USB drive** with no installation required
* **Works on any machine** and just plug in and boot
* **Persistent storage** option to save your settings and apps
* **Take your TV OS anywhere** as your personalized setup travels with you

### 📱 Powerful Remote Control App
* **Complete control** from your smartphone
* **Touchpad mode** for precise cursor movement
* **Keyboard mode** for easy text input from your phone
* **Media controls** including play, pause, skip, volume, and more
* **Quick actions** for Home, Back, Menu, and Power options
* **Directional navigation** with long-press support for faster movement
* **App management** to launch, switch, and close apps remotely
* **Intuitive interface** designed for couch-friendly control

### 🎬 TV-First Experience
* **Fullscreen app launcher** optimized for living room use
* **10-foot UI** designed to be readable from across the room
* **Native and web app support** for maximum flexibility
* **Streaming-ready** with support for all major platforms
* **Family-friendly** interface that anyone can use

## Getting Started

### Requirements
* Any x86_64 computer, even 10+ year old hardware
* USB flash drive with 8GB minimum recommended
* HDMI connection to TV or display
* Keyboard and mouse for initial setup, optional after configuration

### Quick Start
1. Download the LinuxTV ISO
2. Flash it to a USB drive
3. Boot your computer from the USB
4. Enjoy your new TV experience with zero ads and complete freedom
